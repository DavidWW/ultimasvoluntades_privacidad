Últimas Voluntades Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The owner of Últimas Voluntades is committed to protecting the privacy of our app users. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Summary: Últimas Voluntades is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. Your content is stored with end-to-end encryption in your own iCloud account, and we as developers have no access to it.

1. Information We Process

The application processes the following types of information:

  • Phone number: Used to verify your identity via SMS. This process is carried out through Twilio, an external messaging service provider.
  • Content you create: Texts, photos, audio recordings, and documents you choose to save as your last wishes.
  • Recipient contact data: Phone numbers of people you designate as recipients of your messages.

2. Where Your Information is Stored

All your information is stored on Apple's infrastructure:

  • CloudKit (iCloud): Your content is stored encrypted in your own iCloud account, not on our servers.
  • iOS Keychain: Encryption keys are securely stored in your device's Keychain, synchronized through iCloud Keychain.
Important: As developers, we DO NOT have access to your content. Everything is end-to-end encrypted and stored in your personal Apple account.

3. End-to-End Encryption

Últimas Voluntades implements end-to-end encryption (E2E) using:

  • X25519 key exchange
  • AES-GCM encryption for content

This means that only you and the recipients you authorize can access your content. Neither Apple nor we can decrypt your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple CloudKit: For secure storage in iCloud.
  • Apple Push Notifications (APNs): To notify you when someone requests access to your wishes.
  • Twilio: Exclusively for sending SMS verification codes. Twilio does not receive any other personal data.

5. Data We DO NOT Collect

Últimas Voluntades does NOT collect:

  • Analytics or usage data
  • Location information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Data for advertising or marketing
  • Financial or payment information (payments are handled directly by Apple)

6. App Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you if someone requests access.
  • Contacts: To display names instead of phone numbers (optional).
  • Camera/Photos: To attach images to your messages.
  • Microphone: To record voice messages.

All these permissions are optional and you can use the app without granting all of them.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your information remains in your iCloud account as long as you keep the app installed and your account active. You can delete all your content at any time from the app or by deleting iCloud data directly from your device settings.

8. Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive content
  • Mandatory phone verification
  • Storage on Apple's secure infrastructure
  • Private keys protected by iOS Keychain
  • Optional biometric protection (Face ID/Touch ID)

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your information from the app
  • Modify or delete your content at any time
  • Export your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data

10. Minors

Últimas Voluntades is not intended for persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or other means before the changes take effect.
