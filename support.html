Is my data secure?

Yes. All your data is stored with end-to-end encryption (E2E) in your own iCloud account. Not even we can access your content.

How do my recipients access the messages?

Your recipients must download the app and verify their phone number. Basic wishes (disposition, ceremony) will be available immediately if you configured it that way. For personal files, they must request access and wait the time period you set.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

Your data remains in your iCloud, but you'll lose access to premium features (photos, audio, documents, multiple recipients). You can reactivate at any time.

Can I change my recipients?

Yes, you can add, remove, or modify recipients at any time while you have control of your account.
